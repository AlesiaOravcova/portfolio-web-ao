Hi, I'm Alesia! As a junior UX designer, I am thrilled to introduce myself.

Originally from Belarus, I have spent most of my life in Prague.
I have always been curious about people, our differences, and our similarity, so when I discovered UX design, I've got fascinated by how people interact with technology. That's why I chose to pursue a career in UX design. Although I am new to the field, I am eager to learn and grow as a designer.
With a background in finance, I have honed my skills in empathy, collaboration, communication, and logical thinking. These skills have proved valuable in my transition to UX design, where I strive to create user-centered products that meet business goals.
As someone who is naturally curious, I have also gained a basic understanding of frontend development, which has given me an appreciation for the opportunities and limitations of various systems when it comes to producing effective designs.
I am constantly seeking new opportunities to improve my knowledge of UX and UI trends and emerging technologies. I am eager to work with a team of designers and developers to create designs that make a difference in people's lives.
Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me, and I look forward to working with you in the future.
